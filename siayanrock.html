Overview

The SiayanRock Is. website was developed as a course requirement. It serves as an informational site for a hometel located in Batanes, showcasing details such as its purpose, available rooms, and services offered. The project was originally designed as a booking system; however, due to a long period without maintenance, it is currently being updated to realign with its intended purpose.
