GCODE

Overview

GCODE is a simple music-storage playlist mobile application that allows users to add their favorite songs by entering the song title and artist. The app retrieves complete song details—such as the album cover and track duration—using the Spotify API, which is processed through JSON data. I also plan to enhance the project by adding a redirect feature that will let users play the selected song directly when they tap on it.
