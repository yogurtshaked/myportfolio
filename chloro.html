Chloro

Overview

Chloro is a simple monitoring and forecasting application that tracks the quality of your hydroponically grown green leaf lettuce and tells you the best time to harvest. The Chloro system optimizes the Random Forest Regressor model, which analyzes patterns in commonly measured nutrient conditions of hydroponic crops such as pH level, Total Dissolved Solids (TDS), air temperature, and relative humidity.
